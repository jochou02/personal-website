About Me

"Fill the gaps, stay liquid!" - Hondo

In SWAT, "Fill the gaps, stay liquid!" means being adaptable and ready to respond to any situation. This motto perfectly encapsulates my approach to learning and skill development in software engineering. It's about prioritizing depth and fundamentals over sheer breadth.

A professor I had in college instilled in me the importance of mastering fundamentals over chasing the latest fancy framework. I'll admit that I don't know React. But why should I? I've never needed it for my work. Technologies evolve rapidly in this industry, but by focusing on fundamentals like vanilla JS, Python, C++, and Git, I build a solid foundation and remain adaptable. Mastering the basics ensures that learning new technologies and tech stacks becomes a more natural and seamless process, ultimately making me a more versatile and effective engineer.

Why Software Engineering?

Software engineering allows me to combine my analytical skills with creativity. I love the challenge of finding efficient and elegant solutions to problems, and the satisfaction that comes from seeing my code come to life.
